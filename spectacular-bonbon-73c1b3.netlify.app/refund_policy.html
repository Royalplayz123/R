1. Applicable Refunds

Refunds are only applicable if the issue is caused by Galaxy Host (e.g., service not delivered, hardware failure).

  • Service Delivery Failure:
  • Technical Errors:
  • Hardware Failure:
  • Service Unavailability:

If we are unable to provide the service due to an error on our part, you are entitled to a full refund. We will investigate the issue and, if necessary, offer an equivalent service of equal or greater value.

2. Refund Timeframe

Strict window for refund requests.

  • 48-Hour Window:
  • Activation Start:

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of purchase.

The 48-hour period begins the moment your service is activated and you receive your access credentials. No refunds will be issued after 48 hours of service activation.

Refund Requests: All refund requests must be submitted via our Support Ticket system. Requests made via Live Chat, Discord, or Social Media DM will not be accepted.

3. Non-Refundable

Services that are strictly non-refundable.

  • Time Expired:
  • Policy Violation:
  • Misuse:
  • Change of Mind:

No refunds will be issued after 48 hours of service activation.

The following items are NON-REFUNDABLE: No refunds for misuse, plan upgrades/downgrades, or user-requested cancellations.

4. Abuse Policy

Behavior resulting in loss of refund rights.

  • Slurs:
  • Abusive Behavior:
  • Disrespect:
  • Threats:

Abuse toward staff, use of slurs, or threats will result in permanent bans and service termination without refund.

5. Suspension

Conditions under which accounts may be permanently terminated.

  • Payment Disputes:
  • Chargebacks:

Chargebacks or payment disputes will lead to immediate suspension of all services without refund.

Initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment processor constitutes a violation of our Terms of Service. We will immediately suspend all services associated with your account. You will lose all data, and we will not issue a refund under any circumstances.

6. Promotional Services

Rules regarding special pricing or events.

  • Refund Limits:
  • Upgrades:

Promotional, discounted, or special-offer services are non-refundable.

Promotional servers or plans provided at a discounted rate are final sale. No refunds will be issued for these services, even within the 48-hour window, unless there is a technical fault on our end preventing access.
