2. Billing Cycle

Understanding how and when you are charged for your services.

Monthly Billing

  • Duration: Our default billing cycle is 30 days (1 Month).
  • Charges: Your account is automatically charged every month on the same date as your initial invoice.
  • Renewals: If you do not manually cancel your service, it will automatically renew at the end of the billing period.
  • Pro-Rata Refunds: If you upgrade or downgrade your plan mid-cycle, charges will be pro-rated for the remaining days.

Payment Failure

If your payment fails on the due date, we will send you an automated email notification. You have a 7-day grace period to clear the dues before service suspension.

3. Security

We take your financial security extremely seriously.

Encryption

  • All payment pages are secured with 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption.
  • We do not store full credit card numbers. We only store a token or masked last 4 digits for identification.
  • All transactions are processed directly through our PCI-DSS Compliant payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe).

Privacy

  • We will never share your financial details with third parties without your consent.
  • We do not store your payment credentials in plain text. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest.
  • Billing addresses are used solely for invoicing purposes and will not be shared with third parties for marketing.
