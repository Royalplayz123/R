1. Uptime Guarantee

We are committed to maintaining a highly available infrastructure for your services.

3. Exclusions

Scenarios where Galaxy Host is not liable for downtime.

No Compensation For:

  • Scheduled Maintenance: Planned maintenance windows (up to 2 hours) with 48-hour notice are not subject to compensation credits.
  • Third-Party Failures: Outages caused by upstream providers, internet exchange points, or regional power grid failures outside our control.
  • User Configuration Error: Downtime caused by user errors, scripts, or firewall misconfiguration.
  • Abuse or TOS Violation: If service is suspended due to a violation of our Acceptable Use Policy, no compensation will be issued.
  • Force Majeure: Acts of God, war, terrorism, or natural disasters affecting our data centers.
  • DDoS Attacks: While we mitigate attacks, significant volumetric attacks may degrade performance or cause temporary packet loss, which does not qualify for infrastructure downtime compensation.

We strive for perfection and value your uptime.
